About Our Location

Country Kitchen Platteville has been a staple of the community since 1977.

Managed by Chad Holstein since 1997, our restaurant has earned multiple General Manager of the Year awards from Country Kitchen International. Chad has been part of the CK family since 1988.

We're proud to serve home-style meals to families, UW-Platteville students, and visitors from across southwest Wisconsin. Whether it's your first visit or your thousandth, you're family here.

We offer: Dine-in, Carry-out, Delivery, Specialty Coffee, Alcohol, Gift Cards, and Reservations.
